Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sawa English ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses and protects information when you use the Sawa English mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your name and email address if you create an account, along with usage information generated as you use the App, including lesson progress, streaks, and completed exercises. We may also collect basic device information, such as device type and operating system version, to help us maintain compatibility and fix issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate and maintain the App's lessons and features.
  • To track your learning progress across sessions and devices.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers who help us operate the App, such as analytics or crash-reporting tools, and only to the extent needed for them to perform their services. We may also disclose information if required by law.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at the email address below. You may also uninstall the App at any time to stop further data collection.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at sawa.english.app@gmail.com.
